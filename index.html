About

Peter Alcocer is a seasoned industry veteran with over 7 years of experience holding roles in Supply Chain, Manufacturing, and Quality. For the previous 5 years, Peter has had increasing levels of responsibility with the Training space from LMS administration, metrics and data analytics, and instructional design.

With such a wide range in experience, Peter is ready to support any training needs your organization may have. Whether you're organization is a mainstay of the industry or maybe you're still in start up mode, Peter has worked with large organizations like Amgen and Novartis as well as small start ups like AveXis and Atara Biotherapeutics.

Instructional Design

With experience building training in the full Articulate 360, Adobe suite, or PowerPoint for ILTs Peter can create complex learnings solutions to fit the needs of any training program whether it's fully virtual, blended, or in person.

Training Data Analytics

With a strong expertise in data analytics and visualization, Peter can work with stakeholders to help develop meaning, easy to understand metrics for driving continous improvements.

LMS Adminstration

Peter has experience working in many different Learning Management Systems including Sum Total, ComplianceWire, Conerstone on Demand, Trackwise Salesforce LMS, and Veeva. Peter has also lead migrations between systems and has an extensive background in system administration, training/curricula management, user management, HRIS/EDMS feeds, and in depth reporting.

Curriculum Design

Peter has had years of experience creating role based curricula for large scale companies, site start ups, and complete revamps of curricula design. This role based curricula approach is what is needed to created targeted qualification programs, providing the right training to the right staff at the right time.
