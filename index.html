About

Peter Alcocer is a seasoned industry veteran with over 7 years of experience holding roles in Supply Chain, Manufacturing, and Quality. For the previous 5 years, Peter has had increasing levels of responsibility with the Training space from LMS administration, metrics and data analytics, and instructional design.

With such a wide range in experience, Peter is ready to support any training needs your organization may have. Whether you're organization is a mainstay of the industry or maybe you're still in start up mode, Peter has worked with large organizations like Amgen and Novartis as well as small start ups like AveXis and Atara Biotherapeutics.
